Setup Instructions

Gmail

  1. Click "Copy Signature" above
  2. Open Gmail → Settings (gear icon)
  3. Click "See all settings"
  4. Scroll to "Signature" section
  5. Click in the signature box and paste (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V)
  6. Click "Save Changes"

Outlook (Web)

  1. Click "Copy Signature" above
  2. Open Outlook → Settings (gear icon)
  3. Go to Mail → Compose and reply
  4. Under "Email signature", paste your signature
  5. Click "Save"

Apple Mail

  1. Click "Copy Signature" above
  2. Open Mail → Settings → Signatures
  3. Click "+" to create a new signature
  4. Paste your signature in the preview area
  5. Assign it to your email account
